Transportation

According to WorldBank.org:

  • Around the world, a large proportion of urban and inter-urban transport systems remain inaccessible to persons with disabilities
  • Mobility constraints are a serious impediment to disability-inclusive development, as they exacerbate the personal, economic, and social isolation of persons with disabilities
  • To break this cycle, the World Bank's transport experts are helping client countries deliver transport infrastructure and services that can be accessible to all users, including those with disabilities

On this page, organize by subsection, you can find various transportion services in your area:

Ride Share

  • Uber

    uberWAV supports riders who use wheelchairs or scooters that would like to travel to a specific location for a reasonable price. Drivers are certified to assist and keep the riders safe.

    Find Us Here

  • Lyft

    Lyft is a service similar to Uber, but you must have the mobile app to request a ride. You can change your app settings to enable the Access Mode, which will request vehicles that accommodate wheelchair riders.

    Find Us Here

Public Transportation

  • Valley Metro

    Valley Metro provides buses and a light rail that can be used by the public. Their vehicles are made to allow people who use wheelchairs to board with the assistance of employees from Valley Metro.

    Find Us Here

  • Dial-a-Ride

    If a disability prevents people from utilizing the public transportation provided by Valley Metro, people may use Valley Metroâ€™s Dial-a-Ride service to request a bus to their specific location. A Personal Care Attendant must accompany the rider.

    Find Us Here

Personal Transportation

  • National Mobility Equipment Dealers Association

    Asists users with disabilities to find car dealerships that have vehicles which support the use of wheelchairs.

    Find Us Here

  • Valley Medical Supplies

    Valley Medical Supplies allows people to rent wheelchairs and transport chairs for a low price in Phoenix Arizona. The organization will assist in determining the right size of the wheelchair or transport chair.

    Find Us Here
